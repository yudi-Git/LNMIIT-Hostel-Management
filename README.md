# LNMIIT-Hostel-Management
LNMIIT Hostel Management mobile app
This android based mobile app has be developed to make the management of LNMIIT Hostels much more efficient by facilitating
the hostel residents to submit complaints pertaining to various departments, to tac the status of their complaints, submit
complaints anonymously  & to view the important notices postel by the hostel staff.
The app also lets the Hostel Staff to review the complaints submitted by the hostel residents & to post notices related to
hostel matters.
